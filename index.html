Metroid NES (With Custom Level). Created with Xiao-Ping Huynh

This is a remake of the first part of Metroid (1986) by Nintendo for the NES. Xiao and I also created a custom level that uses a gravity flip mechanic.

Controls

  • Movement: arrow buttons
  • Turn into a ball (need morph powerup): down arrow
  • Shoot: x
  • Jump: z
  • Switch Weapons (need missile launcher): Left-shift
  • Flip Gravity in Custom Level (need powerup): Space
  • Pause Game: Enter

Roshambo

Roshambo is a 2D fighting game where two players try to counter each other’s attacks using the Rock-Paper-Scissors advantage triangle in order to turn their opponent’s attacks back at them. Watch out for Clashes, which are classic Rock-Paper-Scissors showdowns that can drastically alter the outcome of a match. Requires two controllers to player (Xbox 360, Xbox One, and PS4 controllers work).

Spliced (Pre-Alpha)

Spliced is a 2D 2v2 fighting game where one player on each team controls the upper half of a character and the other controls the bottom.
